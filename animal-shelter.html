Animal shelter

This webpage is about abandoned animals but rescued by a small farm owners firm. Its located 5km south of Helsinki city in a place called salo.The firm started act of hosting abandoned animals in the early 80s. There are alot of differnt animals hosted in the barn house such as dogs, cats, sheep, horses and rabbits. Some few rescued animal profiles wll be listed on these page.

Liila

Meet Liila, a sheep dog. Liila is a landrace breed of dog and traditionally used by the local shepherds to help them take care of their cattle and sheep. It was found abandoned by its owners on the cold winter season. It was rescued by some people who found her barking for help. It was shivering from the extrem cold which was -9 degree celiese. Now after six months of continous care is in good condition and ready for an adoption.

Kitty

kitty is a cat who was born without eyes, but never considers herself any different from other cats. To Kitty, life is good and he surely lives it to the fullest.
